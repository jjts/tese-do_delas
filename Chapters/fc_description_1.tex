 \Acp{FPGA} are increasingly used in space applications, where radiation-induced \acp{SEU} can corrupt both architectural state and configuration memory in \ac{RISC-V}-based platforms. This chapter frames the problem, highlighting the lack of end-to-end platforms to integrate and validate \acp{FTM} under controlled \ac{FI}, and proposes a solution. A summary of contributions and a brief outline of the remaining chapters are provided.
